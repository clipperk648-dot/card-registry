Frequently Asked Questions

Is my card information safe? +
Yes, your card information is encrypted and protected. We use industry-standard SSL/TLS encryption to ensure your data is secure. We never store sensitive card details permanently.
How often can I check my balance? +
You can check your balance as many times as you want, 24/7. There are no limits on how many checks you can perform. Your history is stored securely for reference.
What if my card number has a different format? +
Our system accepts 16-digit alphanumeric card numbers. The form will automatically format your input as you type. Just enter the numbers and letters without spaces.
Can I export my card history? +
Yes! Once you access the Settings & History section with your password, you can export all your card data as a CSV file that you can open in Excel or any spreadsheet application.
